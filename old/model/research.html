During my PhD, I worked on results concerning projections of Euclidean lattices with applications to coding for continuous alphabet sources. Recently, I became interested in bounds for the reliability of distributed storage systems. In a broad sense, I am interested in the study of Discrete Geometry problems with potential applications to communications.
